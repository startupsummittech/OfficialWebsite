Pravartan

Pre-Event Workshop

Pravartan is the official pre-workshop leading into Thalir’25 – Startup Pitch Arena, designed to equip aspiring innovators with the tools, mindset, and skills they need to pitch like professionals. Scheduled ahead of the grand event, Pravartan ensures that only the most prepared, confident, and visionary student entrepreneurs take the stage at Thalir’25.

This exclusive workshop is open only to selected teams who qualify through the registration and screening process. Participants must also confirm their slot by completing the pitch training fee, which secures their access to curated mentorship, hands-on exercises, and insider insights from startup experts.

When

September 19, 2025

Full Day Workshop

Where

Directorate of Entreprenuership and Innovation

SRMIST, Chennai

Students from all disciplines

Only the shortlisted teams from the Thalir'25 Round-1 will be allowed to attend Pravartan.

Team Size

Each team can have up to 4 members.

8 hours intensive workshop

The Workshop Experience

Pravartan isn’t just another orientation — it’s a simulation of the real startup world. Over the course of the workshop, participants will:



Learn the art of storytelling in pitching — how to hook investors, inspire audiences, and make your idea unforgettable.

Understand the core structure of a winning pitch deck — problem, solution, market, business model, impact, and scalability.

Practice delivering under pressure with mock pitching rounds and expert feedback.

Gain clarity on aligning innovations with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and government mission priorities

Explore dos and don’ts of startup pitching, drawn from real-world success stories and failures.



The environment is interactive, high-energy, and challenge-driven. Every participant will be pushed to think sharper, explain clearer, and present bolder.

Why Participate?

By joining Pravartan, you’re not just preparing for Thalir’25 — you’re building a foundation for your entrepreneurial journey:


  • Learn from mentors who have built and scaled ventures.
  • Refine your startup idea into a pitch-ready solution.
  • Boost confidence and stage presence before facing the Thalir’25 jury.
  • Stand out in the competition with a polished, validated pitch.
  • Exclusive access — only trained participants from Pravartan will qualify to pitch at Thalir’25.

Team Structure

Each team can have up to 4 members.

Teams must undergo a screening process by submitting their ideas in the first phase of registration in Thalir to ensure readiness.


The Bigger Picture

Pravartan is where preparation meets transformation. It is your first step towards the Thalir’25 Startup Pitch Arena — and a chance to turn an idea into an opportunity. Those who walk through Pravartan leave not just as participants but as pitch-ready innovators, capable of making a mark on the startup stage.

Ready to Transform Your Ideas?

Join Pravartan and kickstart your entrepreneurial journey with hands-on learning and expert mentorship.

Only Thalir'25 Finalists will be allowed to attend Pravartan. Participation in the Pravartan Capacity Building Workshop will automatically open for eligible candidates once the Thalir’25 finalists are announced.

Thalir

Startup Pitch Competition

Thalir’25 is SRMIST’s flagship startup pitching platform, held as part of the StartUp Summit 1.0 on 23rd and 24th September 2025. The Thalir’25 pitch event itself will take place on 23rd September 2025, bringing together the most promising student innovators to showcase their game-changing ideas.

This platform is dedicated to deep-tech innovations that address real-world challenges and align with Government missions and Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Ideas in areas such as AI, IoT, robotics, cleantech, biotechnology, and other transformative technologies are encouraged. However, purely Android-based or solely EdTech-focused concepts will not be accepted, ensuring a wide variety of impactful, scalable, and unique solutions.

When

September 23, 2025

Where

TP-2 702

SRMIST, Chennai

Team Size

2-4 Members per Team

Registration Fee: ₹600

The Event Experience

Thalir’25 is designed to feel like stepping into the beating heart of the startup world. From the moment participants take the stage, they enter an environment charged with energy, curiosity, and innovation. Every pitch is an opportunity — not just to impress the jury but to inspire an audience of peers, mentors, and future collaborators.

Teams will face a panel of seasoned entrepreneurs, industry leaders, and investors who will challenge them to refine their ideas, justify their decisions, and think critically under pressure. The atmosphere is competitive yet collaborative — encouraging bold thinking, creative problem-solving, and professional growth.

Beyond the competition, Thalir’25 is a rare chance to network with changemakers, exchange ideas with like-minded innovators, and get firsthand insights into the realities of entrepreneurship. The experience sharpens your ability to pitch, enhances your adaptability, and builds the confidence to navigate the unpredictable world of startups.

Why Participate?

  • Gain real-world validation of your idea from experts in the field
  • Build connections with investors, mentors, and innovators
  • Enhance your pitching and presentation skills
  • Earn recognition in a competitive innovation ecosystem
  • Stand a chance to win exciting rewards and opportunities that can accelerate your entrepreneurial journey

Team Structure

Each team can have up to four members. Forming a multidisciplinary team — combining skills from different technical, academic, or creative fields — is strongly encouraged and can significantly increase your chances of standing out in the competition.

Thalir’25 is where vision meets opportunity — a place for ideas to sprout, grow, and inspire. Whether you walk away with prizes or a network of valuable connections, you’ll leave with skills, experiences, and insights that can shape your entrepreneurial journey.

Tharanga Sangamam – Panel Discussion

Where Ideas Meet Experience and Innovation Sparks Inspiration

What is a Panel Discussion?

A panel discussion is a structured conversation where a group of experts share their insights, experiences, and opinions on a specific theme in front of an audience. The discussion is guided by a moderator, who asks thought-provoking questions, keeps the flow engaging, and ensures each panelist gets to share their perspective.

Unlike a lecture, panel discussions are dynamic and interactive offering different viewpoints, real-life experiences, and sometimes even friendly debates. For the audience, it's an opportunity to gain diverse insights in one place, ask questions, and leave with ideas they can apply in their own journey.
